The Plan

The 30-Day Relaunch Plan

This is the exact framework I use to relaunch expired listings. Read it, use it, and book a call if you’d like me to tailor it to your home.

Week 1

Fresh photography & pricing review

  • Walk-through and punch list of small prep items that move the needle on price
  • Professional listing photography, interior, exterior, and twilight when it helps
  • Comparative market analysis using the most recent 90 days of activity
  • Repositioned price strategy tied to real buyer behavior in your neighborhood
  • New listing copy written for the buyers who are actually shopping right now
Week 2

Targeted marketing launch

  • Coming-soon campaign to warm up the market before we go live
  • Syndication across Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, and the Keller Williams network
  • Paid social campaign targeted to move-up and relocation buyers in Central Ohio
  • Email push to my active buyer database and the Keller Williams agent network
  • Branded single-property website and short video walk-through
Week 3

Open house & buyer outreach

  • Broker open to build agent awareness and feedback
  • Weekend public open house with personal follow-up on every visitor
  • Direct outreach to buyers who toured competing homes in your price range
  • Weekly showing and feedback report delivered every Sunday
  • Pricing check-in if showing activity does not match the plan
Week 4

Offer review & close

  • Side-by-side comparison of every offer and your net proceeds
  • Negotiate price, inspection items, and timeline in your favor
  • Coordinate inspection, appraisal, title, and closing so nothing slips
  • Weekly status updates through the close date
  • Smooth hand-off to your next chapter
Why homes don’t sell the first time

It’s almost never the home.

Nine times out of ten, a listing that didn’t sell was close. One of these four levers was off.

Pricing

Set to last year’s market instead of the buyer activity happening right now in your neighborhood.

Marketing exposure

Limited reach. The right buyers never saw it, or saw it in the wrong places.

Photography

Photos that did not do the home justice. Buyers scroll past every day because of this alone.

Timing

Launched into the wrong week, wrong season, or right after a rate change. All recoverable with a smart relaunch.
